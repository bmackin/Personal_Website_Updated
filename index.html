ABOUT ME

I think a lot about design. I always have I suppose. I've always been curious about how things work, why they work, and if they don't work, what is the better way? I love to find creative ways to solve problems, it's just what I do, both professionally and personally.

I've had an exciting and fulfilling work experience so far, starting out from design school as a signmaker, a designer for an amazing and talented Canadian animation company, learned all about pre-press as the art director for a Cambridge, Ontario print shop, and more recently, I've learned all about editorial design and publishing while doing work for newspapers and magazines, print and digital.

Sounds great right? It was! Problem is, there is not much work going around in the print industry, sadly. So what's a girl to do? I needed to update my skills so I could continue to find work as a designer, obviously, I thought. But in what way? So I turned my eye to a little hobby I had, coding. I had always wanted to learn how to build the things I design.

How exciting! But how do I do this? Going back to school is a huge time commitment, and who can afford to take a year off your life when you have mortgage and car payments and marina fees for your boat?

And then I found Bitmaker Labs and their 9 week Web Development Immersive Program. Perfect! So off I went, had an amazing time, learned so much, and now here I am, looking for a chance to use the new skills I have, and to be able to contribute in return my years of experience as a designer.

Please check out my previous work, git hub, or resume links if you would like to learn more.
